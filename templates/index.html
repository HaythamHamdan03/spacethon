HOW OUR AI WORKS
A simple step-by-step explanation for everyone
01
📡
SATELLITE SENDS DATA
Every minute, the NASA SMAP satellite sends readings from all its sensors to Earth. We use 25 sensor channels simultaneously — like checking 25 vital signs at once.
25 FEATURES PER TIMESTEP
02
🧠
AI LEARNS NORMAL
We fed the AI 2,880 minutes of confirmed normal satellite operation. It learned what healthy looks like — without ever being shown what a fault looks like.
UNSUPERVISED LEARNING
03
⏱️
READS 50 MINUTES AT ONCE
The LSTM looks at a sliding window of 50 consecutive minutes. It doesn't just see the current reading — it remembers what happened before. Like a doctor who knows your history.
LSTM · TEMPORAL MEMORY
04
🔄
TRIES TO RECONSTRUCT
The Autoencoder compresses the data and tries to rebuild it. If the data looks normal — it rebuilds perfectly. If something is wrong — it fails and the error spikes.
AUTOENCODER ARCHITECTURE
05
📊
ERROR = ANOMALY SIGNAL
The failure to reconstruct IS the anomaly score. When the error stays above 3 standard deviations for 10+ consecutive minutes — the system raises an alarm.
3σ THRESHOLD · 10-MIN FILTER
06
⚡
AUTONOMOUS ACTION
No human needed. The system triggers alerts, logs the fault, and initiates backup systems — all in real time, 24/7, with no fatigue or delay.
REAL-TIME · AUTONOMOUS
